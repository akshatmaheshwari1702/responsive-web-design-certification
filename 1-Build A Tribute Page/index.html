Harmonic Science

A musician and developer.

Biography:

Jonathan Souza aka Harmonic Science is a multi-instrumentalist, composer, songwriter and music producer. In 2009 self-taught music production techniques and performed as a DJ in electronic music events, doing DJ-sets and live acts. Along the way, he was awarded with the 3rd place at the online Remix Contest promoted by the band Celldweller. Since then, he has been searching for how he could innovate in music but has never been able to find something that could completely match his artistic expectations. Back in 2016, he took some piano lessons and created a new music project where he tried to innovate, wrote poems and recorded some improvisations with piano, voice, and guitar. Back in early 2017, he showed some of his compositions to friends and family, which turned out to some very good and inspiring feedbacks that motivated him to carry on and compose new songs.
He describes his songs as:
"A drawing of all my feelings poured into music."
